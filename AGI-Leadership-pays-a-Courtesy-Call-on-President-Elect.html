AGI Leadership Pays a Courtesy Call on President Elect - HE John Dramani Mahama

  • AGI
  • December 20, 2024

AGI Leadership Pays a Courtesy Call on President Elect - HE John Dramani Mahama AGI Council led by the President, Dr Humphrey Ayim Darke paid a courtesy call on the President-elect of the Republic of Ghana, His Excellency John Dramani Mahama at his office at the Cantonment. The visit was to congratulate the Presiden-elect for winning the election and also to pledge the AGI's support for programmes and projects that will propel industrial and economic growth.

HE John Dramani Mahama assured the AGI of his commitment to working with Captains of Industry and the private sector to ensure economic growth and prosperity for all.

Council members who participated in the visit included Mr Seth Twum-Akwaboah, CEO, Mrs Grace Amey-Obeng, Vice President for SMEs, AGI, Mr Ralph Ayitey, National Treasurer, AGI, Mr Mukesh Thakwani, National Executive Member of AGI, Dr James Asare-Adjei, Past President, Dr Tony Oteng Gyasi, Past President, Dr Kofi Kludjeson, Past President, Mr Tsonam Akpeloo, Accra Regional Chair, Dr David Ofosu-Dortey, Mr Eric Defor,

Other members were Mr Chris Wulff-Caeser, MD of Unilever, Mr Richard Adjei, CEO of Kasapreko Company Limited, Mr Reggie Sackey-Addo, General Manager for Irani Brothers, Mr Henry Malm, Head of Corporate Affairs, Unilever, Mr Jojo Quayson, Public & International Relations Manager
