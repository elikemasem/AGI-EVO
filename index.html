Sectors

Explore the diverse sectors we serve, including manufacturing, agriculture, technology, and tourism. We focus on fostering growth and collaboration, providing tailored support to help businesses navigate their unique challenges. Join us in driving innovation and ensuring sustainable development in your sector

Chemicals

Focuses on the production and distribution of chemicals used in various industries, including agriculture, manufacturing, and pharmaceuticals. It plays a vital role in supporting industrial growth and innovation.

Export

Involves the sale of goods and services to international markets, contributing to foreign exchange and boosting the local economy. This sector promotes trade relations and diversifies income sources for the nation.

Energy

Involves the generation, distribution, and management of power sources such as electricity, renewable energy, and fossil fuels. This sector is critical for supporting industrial activity, infrastructure development, and sustainable growth.

Oil and Gas

A key sector driving economic growth through exploration, production, and distribution of petroleum products. It includes upstream, midstream, and downstream activities that fuel energy needs and generate significant revenue.

Garments and Textiles

Encompasses the manufacturing of clothing and fabrics, supporting local employment and trade. It contributes to the economy through both domestic sales and export, with a focus on quality production and fashion trends.

Hospitality and Tourism

Encompasses services like hotels, restaurants, travel, and recreation, aimed at enhancing visitor experiences. It contributes significantly to economic growth by attracting domestic and international tourists, creating jobs, and promoting cultural exchange.

Who We Are

Mission: Our mission is to carry out proactive support services to the industrial sector with the view to contributing substantially to the growth and development of industry in Ghana.

Vision To contribute substantially to the growth and development of Industry through effective policy advocacy and provision of business support services. To be the number one voice for Industry by growing a strong industrial base with key sectors that are globally competitive.

Strategic Planning

We believe in the power of foresight and preparation. By setting clear goals and developing strategic plans, we ensure that industries in the Eastern, Volta, and Oti regions have a structured path to growth
